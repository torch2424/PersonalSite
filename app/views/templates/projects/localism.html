• Localight

    Localight is a mobile giftcard and loyalty program for small businesses. It used user's phones to request giftcards through text message, which would then bring them to a mobile web-app to send and receive giftcards from their friends. Localight was developed by Localism in downtown Long Beach, CA. However, the app was taken down as Localism went through some restructuring after I had left.
