Android Development

I developed all of these android apps using the native Android Studio IDE. This allows for better/smoother functionality of the app since it is created with native Android tools. However, I am currently also developing with the Ionic framework to create multiplatform applications. All of which, could be found on my Github.

Feather

“...A bare bones player without the bells and whistles which may be what a lot of people are looking for.” - Android Authority , Joe Hindy , 11 best Android video player apps

“[This] app is the only one I have installed for playing local music and video files, on both my phone and tablet.” - Emi Hardekyn

Looking for a music or video player that isn't slow, always crashing, or freezing your phone? One that allows you to find what you want to listen to or watch through a file explorer, instead of scanning your entire external storage, just to not find everything you have on there? Tired of external codecs, and just want the native ones that are much more reliable and fast? Do you find full screen video controls getting in the way of your video, and you think that they are annoying? Well, Feather was created for just these reasons, and it is presented in a minimal, beautiful, and easy to use format. Where it is all quite reliable, and always quick.



Stats Monitor Widget

Looking for a Conky like, text based, system monitor for android? Tired of slow system monitor widgets making your beautiful phone look ugly, and want something better? Stats Monitor was created for exactly that. As an android user, I created this app off of my own needs and desires as an android user, to create minimalist, text based widget, that could show what's going on in your phone, while keeping the home screens looking beautiful and customizeable.



Trust In Heart (Demo)

A Demo for the indie RPG / JRPG Trust In Heart! This game is an fun and epic adventure of a weak hero that must overcome the king of monsters, to save his love and bring back the peace to the world!



Decicisons

Are you indecisive? And, have trouble making decisions? Do you often ask for someone else's opinion on what you should do when you need to decide something? Decisions was created just for this. It is a customizable, easy to use, and lightweight app that allows you to answer your questions with the help of your phone!
This is all done in 3 easy steps:
1. Enter your question
2. Enter the answers to your questions
3. Open up your new decision, and press decide!



Txtnote

Are you looking for a fast, minimal, smooth notepad app? Or is your current notes app slow or ugly? TxtNote was created with that in mind. TxtNote is a very fast and smooth note taking app that gets what you want done with a very simple and minimalistic design.
